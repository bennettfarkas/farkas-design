**THE FOUNDATION**

# **The Member Reserve Program. Where it all begins.**

*The effective annual value of Member Reserve Program participation? Approximately 16–17% when you account for the time value of money. That’s the published math — and we want you to check it.*

The Member Reserve Program is where your money goes into a secure corporate reserve, stays completely yours, and generates benefit for you while it’s there.

Every dollar you contribute is tracked as Points. Each contribution triggers a Boost Payment funded from CoSpark’s corporate revenue. Not from your reserve. From the corporate capability that your collective participation makes possible. That Boost is sent to your Boost Card — a prepaid Visa you can use today. Groceries. Gas. The electric bill.

You contribute on your own terms: as little as $10 at a time, as often as you’d like. There’s no fixed monthly amount and no required schedule. Some people contribute every week. Some once a month. Some when they can. The pace is yours.

At 60 months, you redeem your full reserve plus a 5% maturity bonus. When you factor in the Boost Payments you’ve been receiving along the way and account for the time value of money, the effective annual value of your participation works out to approximately 16–17%. That number is real. The calculation is published. And we’d love for you to sit down at a Center and walk through it.

That’s a real commitment — five years. And if you need to leave before that, you get your contributions back net of Boost Payments received. No penalties. No fees. The maturity bonus is forfeited, but you walk away with what’s yours. Nobody’s trapped.

**Death Benefit Exchange (DBX)**

*Unlock the value in what you already own.*

If you hold a whole-life insurance policy, you have a real asset — but the utility is locked in the future. DBX lets you convert a portion of that death benefit into present-day ownership value inside the CoSpark commonwealth. Your policy stays intact between you and your insurer. The terms are documented, notarized, and administered through approved custodianship. At claim time, your beneficiary remits the agreed exchange amount and receives the corresponding shares. Clear structure. No hype.

**FROM THE COMMUNITY**

# **“We stopped treading water.”**

Maria teaches third grade. David runs a small landscaping business. They showed up at the Colorado Springs Center on a Saturday morning because someone at David’s church mentioned it. They weren’t in crisis — just stuck. Doing the right things and still feeling like they were running to stay in place.

They started with the Member Reserve Program. A contribution they could handle. Within a few months, the Boost Payments were showing up on their Boost Cards. David used his for work boots. Maria covered a week of groceries. Their reserve kept growing, untouched.

Eighteen months in, David’s participation history qualified him for equipment financing on terms his bank had never come close to offering. He bought a second service van and hired his first part-time crew member that spring. Maria signed up for coaching and started building a tutoring idea she’d had for years.

They didn’t need a windfall. They needed a system that recognized what they were already willing to do.

# **Common Questions**

**Is this too good to be true?**

The Boost Payment comes from corporate revenue generated by pooled reserves. The mechanics are published and the math is straightforward. Come to a Center and we’ll walk through it with you.

**What if I need to leave early?**

Your contributions are redeemable net of Boost Payments received. No penalties. No fees. The maturity bonus is forfeited, but you leave with what’s yours.

**Is CoSpark a bank?**

No. CoSpark is a member-driven community operated by The Community Corp. Not a bank, credit union, or investment vehicle. Programs, terms, and availability are described in the membership agreement.

**Do I need good credit?**

No. The Member Reserve Program is open to anyone ready to participate. Your credit history isn’t a factor.
