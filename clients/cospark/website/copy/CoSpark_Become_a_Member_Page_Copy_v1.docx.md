# **Two tiers. Transparent pricing. No surprises.**

**Associate Membership — $8.88/month**

Full access to the CoSpark community, education programming, Center events, and the Member Reserve Program. Annual option: $106.56/year.

**Premium Membership — $25/month**

Everything in Associate, plus the ability to list in the Member Marketplace, submit Initiatives for approval, and access mentoring programs. Premium is for members ready to use the full range of CoSpark pathways. Annual option: $300/year.

Both tiers are available for Individual and Organization accounts. Every dollar of your membership supports the infrastructure that makes the community work — Centers, programming, and the systems that keep participation transparent and accountable.

Membership fees convert to equity shares in CoSpark every three years. You are building ownership, not renting access.

# **Straight answers.**

**Can I cancel?**

Yes. Membership can be canceled at any time by either the member or CoSpark.

**Do I need to live near a Center?**

No. CoSpark operates online and through virtual programming. Centers enhance the experience, but membership is not location-dependent.

**What is the difference between Associate and Premium?**

Associate gives you full access to the community, education, Centers, and the Member Reserve Program. Premium adds Marketplace listing, Initiative submission, and mentoring programs. You can start as Associate and upgrade at any time.

**Is this a bank?**

No. CoSpark is a private, member-driven community structured as a C-Corp. It is not a bank, credit union, or investment fund. It does not offer FDIC-insured products. The model is built on participation and shared economic activity, not on traditional financial services.

**How do I know this is legitimate?**

The model is published. The formulas are available. The leadership is accessible. Visit a Center, attend a workshop, or talk to a team member. CoSpark was built to answer hard questions, not avoid them.
