CoSpark Centers are professional work studios where the commonwealth becomes tangible. Members come to attend workshops, meet mentors, collaborate on Initiatives, explore lending pathways, and connect with other members building in their local area. For prospective members, Centers are the front door: a place where the skeptic’s questions get answered face to face.

What you’ll find at a Center:

**Workshops and education —** Financial literacy, business coaching, and community programming led by practitioners who have built what they teach. Sessions are designed for meaningful conversation across industries, experience levels, and business stages.

**Collaboration and workspace —** A place to work alongside other members, find partners for Initiatives, connect with local businesses in the network, and build the relationships that make the commonwealth more than a concept.

**Community events —** Regular gatherings that bring members and neighbors together. Some are educational. Some are social. All of them exist because trust is built in person, not on a screen.

**The BrightEyed Society —** A breakfast-forward restaurant integrated into the Center. Good food and shared tables. The restaurant operates as part of the community rhythm — a place where members and visitors cross paths naturally.

**TRUST YOU CAN WALK INTO**

# **Trust is built in person.**

Walk in, sit down, and ask every question you have. Meet the people who are already participating. Watch a workshop, have breakfast, stay as long as you need.

Centers exist because trust is built when you can look someone in the eye and get a straight answer. The model is documented, the formulas are published, and the math is transparent — but none of that matters as much as sitting across from a member who can tell you what their experience has actually been.

That is why Centers are foundational to CoSpark. They are where the commonwealth becomes local, where participation has a physical heartbeat, and where the community keeps itself honest.

**THE FIRST CENTER**

# **Colorado Springs. The prototype.**

The first CoSpark Center is in Colorado Springs, Colorado where CoSpark was born. It is the working prototype for every Center that will follow.

The Colorado Springs Center houses the BrightEyed Society restaurant, workshop and event space, collaborative work areas, and the local team that supports members in the region. It is open to both members and visitors. You do not need to be a member to walk in, attend an event, or have a meal.
