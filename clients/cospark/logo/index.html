Usage Guidelines

Follow these rules to keep the CoSpark brand consistent across all touchpoints.

Clear Space

Maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the spark icon. No other elements should intrude into this area.

Minimum Size

The horizontal logo should not be used smaller than 120px wide on screen or 30mm in print. The icon alone can go as small as 24px.

Color Usage

Use Slate on light backgrounds and White on dark backgrounds. Copper is reserved for accent or emphasis — never as the primary logo color on body text.

Background Contrast

Always ensure sufficient contrast between the logo and its background. Avoid placing the logo on busy images or patterns without a solid backing.

Don'ts

Don't rotate or skew the logo

Don't reduce opacity or make it transparent

Don't change the logo colors outside the palette

Don't stretch or distort the proportions
