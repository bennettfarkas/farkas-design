CoSpark

Homepage Design Directions

Six explorations for the CoSpark homepage. Each takes a different approach to scroll behavior, visual density, and brand expression.

Direction A

Editorial Scroll Cinema

Long-form storytelling with cinematic pacing

Full-viewport hero with layered imagery. Content reveals on scroll with instant triggers. Photography-forward with atmospheric depth. The most editorial of the six.

Photography Scroll Reveals Editorial
Direction B

Kinetic Grid

Construction-line energy with structural precision

12-column overlay grid, DM Mono section numbers, clip-reveal animations. Feels like the blueprints of a financial system being assembled in real time.

Grid System Monospace Clip Reveals
Direction C

Immersive Storytelling

Full-viewport scenes with frosted glass overlays

Ken Burns parallax on full-bleed photography. Frosted glass content panels. Progress indicator dots. The most immersive, cinematic experience.

Full Bleed Ken Burns Frosted Glass
Direction D

Systematic Precision

Jony Ive-inspired clarity with tile system integration

White space dominance, typography as hero. CSS-rendered tile shapes as mathematical compositions. Color enters progressively through the scroll. The most refined and restrained.

Tile System Minimalist Type-Forward
Direction E

Chain Reaction

Dark kinetic energy with fuse-line connections

Slate-950 canvas. Animated spark constellation hero. Vertical fuse lines draw between sections. Color stages from slate to copper to ember to patina as the "reaction" spreads.

Dark Mode Fuse Animation Color Stages
Direction F

Living Mosaic

The tile grid IS the page layout

Photography masked in tile shapes. Mural strips between sections with progressive color palette expansion. Brand tile vocabulary woven throughout as the structural DNA of the page.

Tile System Mosaic Layout Photo Masks
