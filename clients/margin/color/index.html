Usage Guidelines

Rules for consistent, intentional color usage across all touchpoints.

Pine as Foundation

Pine is the default text and background color. It grounds the page and provides the deep, quiet canvas that lets Spring stand out.

Spring as Accent

Spring is for energy, action, and emphasis. Use it for CTAs, highlights, and moments that need to pop. Never set body text in Spring on a light background.

Amber for Caution

Amber signals warnings, pending states, and positive financial movement. Use it for alert banners, gain indicators, and anything that needs attention without alarm.

Scarlet for Danger

Scarlet signals errors, losses, and destructive actions. Use it for validation errors, negative deltas, and confirmations of irreversible operations.

Temperature Balance

Pine and Spring are the cool primary pair. Amber and Scarlet are the warm utility pair. Let the cool pair dominate — warm colors should appear only where their meaning is needed.

Accessibility

Always maintain WCAG AA contrast (4.5:1 minimum) for text. Use the scale steps to find accessible pairings — light text on dark backgrounds, dark text on light.
