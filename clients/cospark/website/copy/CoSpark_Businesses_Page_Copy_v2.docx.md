# **The Member Reserve Program. A resilience layer built for how your business actually works.**

*When you account for the time value of money, the effective annual value of Member Reserve Program participation works out to approximately 16–17%. That’s published math. We want you to check it.*

The Member Reserve Program is where your business contributes to a secure corporate reserve. Your contributions stay completely intact, fully under your control, and every dollar is tracked as Points and generates immediate benefit.

Each contribution triggers a Boost Payment funded from CoSpark’s corporate revenue and delivered directly to your business account via ACH. Not from your reserve. From the corporate capability your collective participation creates. That’s money that improves your cashflow today while your reserve keeps building untouched.

You set the pace. Contribute what makes sense for your business, when it makes sense. The schedule is yours. The minimum is accessible and the structure is flexible enough to accommodate the reality of business cashflow, which is that some months are better than others.

At 60 months, you redeem your full reserve plus a 5% maturity bonus. Factor in the Boost Payments you’ve received along the way and the effective annual value of your participation works out to approximately 16–17%. The formula is published. The math is transparent.

That’s a five-year commitment. And if your business needs to exit early, your contributions are returned net of Boost Payments received. No penalties. No fees. The maturity bonus is forfeited, but you leave with what’s yours.

# **Common Questions**

**Is this like a business savings account?**

No. CoSpark is not a bank. The Member Reserve Program is a participation program — your contributions are held intact in a secure corporate reserve, and the Boost Payments are funded from corporate revenue, not from members’ reserves. Programs, terms, and availability are described in the membership agreement.

**What’s the actual cost to my business?**

Your contributions stay completely yours and are returned at maturity plus a 5% bonus. The Boost Payments you receive along the way are additional value on top of that. There are no hidden fees and no account maintenance charges. The effective annual value of participation, when you account for timing, works out to approximately 16–17%.

**Can my employees participate?**

Yes. Individual employees can join the Member Reserve Program independently through the same program available to individuals and families. It’s a benefit you can promote without it costing your business anything.

**How does this compare to what my bank offers?**

Your bank uses your money to fund loans and keeps the margin. CoSpark shares a portion of the corporate capability your participation creates — through Boost Payments — while your reserve stays intact. The mechanics are published and we’re happy to walk through a side-by-side comparison at any Center.
